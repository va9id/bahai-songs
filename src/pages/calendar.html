Calendars

A Bahá'í Calendar containing all the 19-day Feasts, Holy Days, and other significant dates ready to be added to your Apple, Google, or Outlook calendars. To add the Bahá'í calendar to your calendar refer to the instructions below.

Subscribe

Subscribe to the calendar through the following link to automatically receive any future updates, such as new dates for the next year:
  • webcal://bahaisongs.ca/assets/calendar/bahai_calendar.ics

Download

Manually download the .ics calendar file below and upload it to your calendar app:
  • bahai_calendar.ics
This method will not automatically update the calendar events when changed, whereas subscribing will.
