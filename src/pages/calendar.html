Calendars

A Bahá'í Calendar containing all the 19-day Feasts, Holy Days, and other significant dates ready to be added to your Apple, Google, or Outlook calendars. To add the Bahá'í calendar to your calendar refer to the instructions below.

Subscribe

Subscribe to the calendar to automatically receive future updates, such as new dates for the next year. Instructions to subscribe through different calendar apps are provided below.

Apple Calendar

  1. Open the Calendar app on your iPhone, iPad, or Mac.
  2. Go to Calendars → Add Calendar → Add Subscription Calendar (on Mac: File → New Calendar Subscription)
  3. Copy and paste the following Subscription URL: https://bahaisongs.ca/assets/calendar/bahai_calendar.ics

Google Calendar

  1. Open Google Calendar on your device.
  2. Go to Other Calendars → Add other calendar (+) → From URL
  3. Copy and paste the following URL: https://bahaisongs.ca/assets/calendar/bahai_calendar.ics

Outlook Calendar

  1. Open Outlook on your device.
  2. Go to Calendar → Add Calendar → Subcribe from web
  3. Copy and paste the following URL: https://bahaisongs.ca/assets/calendar/bahai_calendar.ics

Download

The second option is to manually download the .ics calendar file below and upload it to your calendar app:
  • bahai_calendar.ics
This is not the preferred method as it will not automatically update the calendar events when changed, whereas subscribing will.
