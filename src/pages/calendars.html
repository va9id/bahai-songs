Calendars

A Bahá'í Calendar containing all the 19-day Feasts, Holy Days, and other significant dates ready to be added to your Apple, Google, or Outlook calendars. There are two ways to add these calendars in your calendar app that are explained below.

Subscribe

Automatically be subscribed to the calendar, this way when changes are made they will automatically update in your calendar app. To subscribe, navigate to your calendar application and add a new calendar, there will be an option to "Subscribe" to a calendar by entering a URL. Copy and paste the following URL to be automatically subscribed to the calendar:
  • https://bahaisongs.ca/assets/calendars/bahai_calendar.ics

Download

The second option is to manually download the .ics calendar file below:
  • bahai_calendar.ics
After downloading you can upload it to your calendar app through the calendar import feature. This method will not automatically update the calendar events if they are changed, hence it is preferred to subscribe.
